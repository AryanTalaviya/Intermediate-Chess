4. En Passant üëª

The "ghost capture" - a sneaky pawn move that catches opponents off guard!

When can you use En Passant?

  1. Enemy pawn moves 2 squares from starting position
  2. Your pawn is next to it (on same rank)
  3. You capture immediately on your next turn

5. Stalemate ü§ù

When nobody wins! Sometimes the best defense is... no moves at all.

What is Stalemate?

  • It's your turn to move
  • Your king is NOT in check
  • You have NO legal moves
  • Result: Draw (tie game)
